A sports car is a type of car that is designed with an emphasis on dynamic performance, such as handling, acceleration, top speed, the thrill of driving, and racing capability. Sports cars originated in Europe in early 1910s and are currently produced by many manufacturers around the world.

Traditionally, the most common layout for sports cars was a roadster (a two-seat car without a fixed roof),[27] however there are also several examples of early sports cars with four seats.[5]
